# Urban Nomad — Our Ethics

Urban Nomad exists to make exploring any city, near or far, feel welcoming and possible. Here's how we try to live up to that.

## Everyone is welcome here
Travelers, locals, and curious humans of every background, identity, ability, language, faith, and belief — cities belong to all of us. Urban Nomad is built for all of us.

## Zero tolerance for hate
Every community tip is screened by AI moderation before it appears on the site. Content targeting people based on race, religion, gender identity, sexual orientation, nationality, disability, or any other identity is automatically blocked and logged. No exceptions.

## We respect cultures — we don't reduce them
Our AI-generated guides aim to help visitors understand local norms and show respect, not flatten a city's culture into clichés. If you find AI-generated content that misrepresents a place or its people, please tell us so we can improve.

## Privacy by design
- **No accounts. No logins. No tracking.**
- Your saved events and recent searches live only in your browser's storage — never on our servers.
- The only thing we store on our end is the community tips you choose to post.
- We don't sell your data because we don't collect any to sell.

## We're honest about AI
"Your Local Guide" is generated by AI (OpenAI's GPT-4), and it's clearly labeled as such everywhere it appears. AI can be wrong, biased, or out of date. Use it as a starting point, not gospel. Always cross-check anything that matters.

## We're open about how it works
Urban Nomad is open source. The code, the AI prompts, and the moderation rules are all public on GitHub. Found a bug, a bias, or something we should do better? Open an issue.
